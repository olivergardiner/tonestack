The Circuits

The library of circuits included within Tonestack will hopefully continue to expand - the circuits are currently organised in three categories - passive tone stacks (as found in valve guitar amps), active filters for use in a variety of contexts, and basic passive filter circuits as an introduction to simple filter theory

Passive tone stacks

Most discussions of amplifier tone stacks begin with the big three "FMV" stacks - Fender, Marshall and Vox. These stacks are obviously included but, here, the amplifier tone stacks have been organised according to the number of controls available as this is often a key consideration when planning the real estate on the front panel. As a consequence, the Vox tone stack is covered further down the list in the "Two knob" section.
